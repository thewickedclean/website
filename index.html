Discover Wicked Clean

Welcome to Wicked Clean, your premier destination for custom auto detailing and window tinting services nestled in the heart of Alpharetta, Georgia. Our meticulously curated packages cater to all levels of car enthusiasts, from the essentials like a basic wash and wax to the pinnacle of protection with our warrantied ceramic coatings that endure for years.

At Wicked Clean, we take pride in our specialization in high-end ceramic coatings, a service that requires certification for installation. Our team of certified professionals ensures that your vehicle receives the utmost care and attention, leaving it with a stunning, showroom-worthy finish that exceeds your expectations.

Whether you're looking to enhance your car's appearance, protect its paintwork, or improve your driving experience with window tinting, Wicked Clean is here to fulfill your automotive detailing needs with unparalleled craftsmanship and expertise. Experience the difference with Wicked Clean today.

Chris's Journey

Hey there, I'm Chris, and I proudly call Alpharetta, Georgia, my home. From a young age, my fascination with cars and their aesthetics has been a driving force in my life. Over the years, I've honed my skills in car detailing, delighting friends and family with my meticulous work.

As the demand for high-quality detailing services continued to rise, I saw an opportunity to turn my passion into a business venture. In September 2020, I took the leap and opened Wicked Clean in Bettendorf, Iowa. The response was overwhelmingly positive, with customers appreciating the attention to detail and personalized service we offered.

However, as life sometimes dictates, circumstances led me to make the tough decision to close Wicked Clean in 2022 and relocate to Georgia. Despite the challenges, I saw it as an opportunity for growth and a chance to bring my expertise to a new market.

Now, settled in Alpharetta, I'm thrilled to be relaunching Wicked Clean in my new community. With a fresh perspective and the same dedication to excellence, I'm excited to continue providing top-notch detailing services to car enthusiasts in the area. My goal is not just to meet but to exceed the expectations of every customer who walks through our doors.
