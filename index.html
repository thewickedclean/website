Discover Wicked Clean

 

Welcome to Wicked Clean, your premier destination for custom auto detailing and window tinting services nestled in the heart of Alpharetta, Georgia. Our meticulously curated packages cater to all levels of car enthusiasts, from the essentials like a basic wash and wax to the pinnacle of protection with our warrantied ceramic coatings that endure for years.

At Wicked Clean, we take pride in our specialization in high-end ceramic coatings, a service that requires certification for installation. Our team of certified professionals ensures that your vehicle receives the utmost care and attention, leaving it with a stunning, showroom-worthy finish that exceeds your expectations.

Whether you're looking to enhance your car's appearance, protect its paintwork, or improve your driving experience with window tinting, Wicked Clean is here to fulfill your automotive detailing needs with unparalleled craftsmanship and expertise. Experience the difference with Wicked Clean today.
