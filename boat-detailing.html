Boat Detailing Quote Request

Wicked Clean specializes in boat cleaning services with a focus on deck and seat cleaning. Additionally, we offer ceramic coating protection for seats to ensure long-lasting durability and ease of maintenance.

Services Offered

Deck Cleaning

  • Surface Cleaning: Comprehensive cleaning of all deck surfaces to remove dirt, grime, salt deposits, and other contaminants.
  • Teak Cleaning: Specialized cleaning for teak decks to maintain their natural beauty and prevent deterioration.
  • Non-Skid Surface Cleaning: Thorough cleaning of non-skid surfaces to ensure they remain safe and effective.

Seat Cleaning and Protection

  • Vinyl and Upholstery Cleaning: Deep cleaning of all seating areas to remove stains, mildew, and dirt, keeping your seats looking fresh and inviting.
  • Ceramic Coating Protection: Application of high-quality ceramic coatings to seats, providing a protective barrier against UV rays, saltwater, stains, and everyday wear and tear.

Benefits of Ceramic Coating for Seats

  • Enhanced Durability: Ceramic coatings create a strong, protective layer that extends the life of your seats.
  • UV Protection: Shields seats from harmful UV rays, preventing fading and cracking.
  • Stain Resistance: Makes it easier to clean seats and prevents stains from setting in.
  • Water Repellency: Creates a hydrophobic surface that repels water and prevents mold and mildew growth.
